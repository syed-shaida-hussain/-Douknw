Rules

Rule 1 :

For taking a quiz you just need to click on Take Quiz.

Rule 2 :

You will be asked 5 questions for each quiz you take.

Rule 3 :

For each right answer , you will be given five points.

Rule 4 :

For each wrong answer , you will be penalised by five points.

Rule 5 :

You can see your final score at the end of the quiz.
